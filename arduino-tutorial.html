The Following are the Arduino tutorials and resources for learning Arduino at various levels.

Paul McWhorter New Arduino Lessons – A Tutorial for Everyone


This Great Arduino Tutorial by Paul McWhorter is recommended for everyone as the tutorial teaches you the basics and then takes on the complex theory, making you an expert.

You can also check out his Old Arduino Tutorials.
